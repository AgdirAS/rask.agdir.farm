## FAQ

**What counts as a "user"?**
Anyone who logs into Rask counts as a user.

**What happens when my team grows past 9 users?**
You'll need a Pro license. Limits are not technically enforced — it's on you to upgrade.

**Can I self-host on Pro/Enterprise?**
Pro is cloud-hosted. On-premises is Enterprise only.

**Is there a trial for Pro?**
Yes — 14 days free, no credit card required.

**Pricing in USD?**
Available on request — contact sales@agdir.no.

**Are limits technically enforced?**
No. Rask does not phone home, check licenses, or block features based on user count or node count. The limits are defined in our license and enforced by trust, not by code. We believe in honest use — if Rask is valuable to your organization, please support its development with the appropriate plan.
